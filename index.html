A Little About Me

I love playing the piano and making music with my family and friends. I also love the sea creatures in the Pacific Northwest. My Passion and Professional Goals

It all began with a few conversations with a friend working in the bioinformatics field. I was fascinated with how his work combines computer science and biology to help patients. He writes programs using genetic data from children with asthma to discover genes associated with asthma. His results provide potential advancements to preventative medicine. His work inspired me to learn more about programming and its applications in medicine. So here I am!
